About Us

At Active Life, we are dedicated to promoting physical activity and wellness within our community. As a non-governmental organization, we believe that sports play a crucial role in building healthier, stronger individuals and fostering a sense of unity. Through various sporting activities and events, we aim to nspire people of all ages to stay active, mprove their health, and embrace an active lifestyle. Our mission is to create inclusive opportunities for everyone regardless of age or background, to engage in sports, enjoy physical activity, and build lasting connections. Join us on our journey to a healthier and more active world!
